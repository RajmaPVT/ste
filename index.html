Robotics

Mechanical technology is a field of study and innovation that includes the plan, development, activity, and utilization of robots. Robots are machines that can do assignments independently or with negligible human intercession. Advanced mechanics envelops a large number of disciplines including mechanical designing, electrical designing, software engineering, and man-made consciousness.

02

Programming

Programming is the process of creating instructions for computers to follow in order to perform specific tasks. It involves writing code using programming languages, which are sets of syntax and rules that define how the instructions are written. Programmers use these languages to develop software, websites, apps, and other digital solutions.

A.I & ML

"A.I. & ML" typically refers to the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) and Machine Learning (ML). A.I. encompasses the broader field of creating systems that can perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence, while ML is a subset of A.I. focused on algorithms that allow computers to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data.
